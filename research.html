Research Overview

Hi, I am Aditya Ganeshan, currently working as a Researcher at Preferred Networks Inc, Japan. Before this, I worked as a Research Assistant at Video Analytics Lab, IISc, Bangalore, India. My research centers around Deep learning for computer vision and creative applications. Earlier, I was very curious about the stability of deep features, and published 2 works which explore adversarial attacks. Lately, I have been more driven towards traditional Computer Vision problems, such as video analysis, scene reconstruction and semi-supervised learning.

I completed my Integrated Masters of Science in Applied Mathematics from IIT Roorkee in 2017. I am still a young researcher in this field, and look forward to pursuing doctoral research in this field, when the time and place is right!

Some of my current research project are exploring the following topics:

  1. Semi-supervised methods for data efficient learning in autonomous driving.
  2. Handling the long-tail of occlusion in instance segmentation.
  3. Grammar based Scene Parsing for large-scale 3D data.
  4. Application of Deep Learning in VR solutions.

For a complete list of published code, visit Google Scholar.
