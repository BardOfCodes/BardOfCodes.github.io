Research Overview

Hi, I am Aditya Ganeshan, currently working as a Researcher at Preferred Networks Inc, Japan. Before this I worked as a Research Assistant at Video Analytics Lab, IISc, Bangalore, India. My research centers around Deep learning for perception and creative applications.. Earlier, I was very curious about the stability of deep features, and published 2 works which explore adversarial attacks. Lately, I have been more driven towards traditional Computer Vision problems, such as video analysis, scene reconstruction and image rectification. Hold on to your seat belts for a few publications in that direction in the near future!

Listed below are domains (at a very high level) where I would like to make contributions. These are based on the instersection between my skills, my interests, and the impactfulness of the problem.

  1. Perception using Deep learning: Be it robotics, or AR, perception will play a key role.
  2. Procedural Generation: As more and more of human population becomes avid consumers, content generation becomes an increasingly massive problem. Further, generative modelling can assist creative endeavors by assisting search across possibilities.

I completed my Integrated Masters of Science in Applied Mathematics from IIT Roorkee in 2017. I am still a young researcher in this field, and look forward to pursuing doctoral research in this field, when the time and place is right!

My Current Research Goals:

  1. Semi-supervised methods for data efficient learning in autonomous driving.
  2. Scene Parsing for largescale 3D data.
  3. Benefits of using VR at workplace.
